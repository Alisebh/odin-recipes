Egusi

Description

Egusi soup is a known nigerian cuisine and staple food for all nigerians. most people would like to eat htis soup with various cereal swallows like, semolina, wholewheat and our native cassava flakes also known as garri Egusi soup can also be taken with white rice and makes for an exellent alternative fot tomato stews.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup blended melon
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • 1 tbsp. crayfish
  • 1 tbsp. palm oil
  • 2 chopped
  • Chicken
  • Uziza
  • Turkey
  • habanero peppers
  • 1 tbsp. locust beans
  • 1 cup stock
  • 1 tsp. yellow pepper
  • 1 medium sized onion
  • Ugu vegetable
  • 4 medium sized smoked panla fish
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tsp. salt

Steps

  1. Add melon, onion, water and fresh pepper in a blender. Blend th ingredients together until it forms a paste.
  2. Add water, chicken, turkey, ponmo, yellow pepper, stock, smoked panla fish, crayfish, chopped pepper and palm oil to a pot. Allow all the ingredients to boil for 15 minutes.
  3. Now scoop little bits of the egusi paste into the pot. Do not stir in the mixture, just cover the pot and allow it to cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Next add the Ugu leaves and Uziza, mix together and allow to cook for 5 minutes. You will notice that the oil will start to rise to the top, your Egusi Soup is now ready.
